About Our Project

Our URL shortener project is designed to transform long and unwieldy links into concise, easy-to-share URLs. Perfect for social media, marketing campaigns, and more, our tool provides a seamless experience with real-time analytics and tracking.

Key Features

  • Customizable URL Stubs: Personalize each short URL with your choice of text.
  • URL Analytics: Gain insights on link clicks, geographic data, and other metrics to optimize your marketing strategy.
  • Bulk URL Upload: Save time by uploading multiple URLs at once in CSV format.
  • Secure and Reliable: Our shortener ensures secure, encrypted URL management.
